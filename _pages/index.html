Wales

Discover a land of legends this autumn
intro: >

Wales is the stuff of legends.

Craggy peaks and wild weather, ruined castles clinging to precarious summits, tales of lovestruck princesses, of dragons and wizards – Wales is a landscape of drama, irresistible romance, and superlative escapes. Autumn provides a colourful backdrop to your outdoor adventures, framed by shades of copper and maroon, inviting wistful walks and time spent in cosy rural pubs. Discover foodie treats, tear down steep slopes on a mountainbike, or unpick a rugged coastline of ravishing beauty. Meet the people of Wales that make all of this possible, the everyday heroes that ensure each traveller leaves itching to return to this unforgettable land.

Start your Wales wanderlust this autumn, here.
